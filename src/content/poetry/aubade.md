Stay—the dark is only\
the negative of day,\
the room still holding\
the shape of what we said.

Soon the window will declare\
its ordinary miracle:\
the world, again,\
unbearably exact.

Until then, let us be\
the last two things\
the morning has not yet\
decided to reveal.
