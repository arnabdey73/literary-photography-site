description: "On photographs, and the small forevers they make."

A photograph is a held breath,\
the one second the world agreed\
to stop pretending it would last.

Everything in it has already gone—\
the light, the leaf, the turning face—\
and yet here it stays,

proof that we were paying attention,\
that once, in all the rushing,\
we thought a moment worth the keeping.
