In the city's loud machine\
there is a seam of quiet—\
one bench, one breath,\
one figure leaning out of the hurry.

The leaves come down\
the way the mind does\
when it finally stops\
arguing with the day:

slowly, and then\
all at once,\
learning how to fall\
without asking where.
